Xvalues

Xvalues, the latest app from Wine Spectator, tells you exactly what red, white, or sparkling wines to seek out next time you're shopping for a bottle. Whether you are a wine geek or new to the wine world, Xvalues will surface Wine Spectator's most highly rated and easy-to-find wines that don't break the bank.
